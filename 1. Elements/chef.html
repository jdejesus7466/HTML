• Eggs
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl until fully combined
  2. Warm up a pan to a medium heat
  3. Melt butter in the warmed pan
  4. Pour whisked eggs into pan
  5. Once formed, place the cheese, salt and pepper on top of the eggs
  6. Flip the eggs on top of each other and fold
  7. Wait for the cheese to melt
  8. Serve and Enjoy!
